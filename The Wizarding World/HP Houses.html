Hogwarts Houses

Mission

The Wizarding World of Harry Potter empowers its users to experience
a virtual community where friends and family thrive, flourish, and prosper.
To welcome witches and wizards from different regions of the sublime world of
magic and sorcery.

Vision

The Wizarding World of Harry Potter envisions a world in which
magic and sorcery brings wizards and muggles together to establish
camaraderie and love for one another, to banish the borders of
distinction between each and every one, and to buildout the union of all.
